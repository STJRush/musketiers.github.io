Infotainment

Infotainment is a new chip made by multi-million company, Intel. Tesla will use this chip to power their interface for their new Model 3. The chip will power the car's power management, air con units, sound and media systems and Bluetooth connections. The chip will power a 1080p HD screen interface using touch screen as an ease of acess to the user.

Tesla switched from Nvidia Graphics to use the powerful Intel IVI system.
